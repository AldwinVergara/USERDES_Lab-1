Invitation Design Activity

Aldwin D. Vergara | October 15, 2020

1) How can we improve this? You can point out details and give comments for improvements.

The picture above is an example of a poor design for an invitation letter. By reading the details (title, what, when, where, and remarks) present in the image, one can already get and understand the objective or the idea of the letter. But the wordings, format, and the overall design defeats the purpose of the said invitation letter. To start off, an invitation letter should not be as plain as this example. It would be more appealing and inviting if there would be a background, colors, and other graphic designs (at least simple designs). Other than the overall design, the texts or the details are the most important part of this letter. It should be again, have at least a design (color, font-style, font-size, textdecoration) that would match the overall concept or theme of the event or the invitation letter. Alignments and spacing should also be observed. Make sure that everything is aligned accordingly depending on the format not left-aligned like the example above. One should also take note of proper capitalization or letter cases. The ‘w’ in when and ‘j’ in June is not capitalized. And every first word from the start of a sentence should have an upper case letter. And one should also be mindful about the details to be included in the letter. Choice of words should be kept in check around these types of letters. For me, I would change the word ‘nibbles’ to ‘snacks’ since it’s a general term that more people can understand, and the reservation part could have been said in a better way but still leaving the same meaning or purpose. Make sure that every important detail of the event are stated well and should not let the receivers of the letter ask a question. Every question should already be answered in the invitation letter, say the venue of the party only mentioned “the pad” which can be any other “the pad” around the city. The address or a mini map of the place would be sufficient to make sure that your guests arrived safe at the correct venue. It would be also better for an invitation if the organizers included their contact information to let guests know who will they contact for assistance. Overall, check the design, theme, details, and formality for invitations like these are crucial for these types of events.


2) What would be the objective function? Tell me what the passage is all about. What is the objective?

The general objective function of the passage is to invite guests to Robert and Alexandra’s after-dinner party. Along the invitation, the passage also tells the guests what the event is all about, and when and where the event will take place. Other than the passage’s objective function to invite, this invitation letter also serves as a way for the organizers of the event to know and count how many guests are expected to come to their event. The invitation will give a responsibility to the guests to confirm with the organizers that their schedule is free for the event. Should the guests confirm their availability, the organizers would reserve them their seats to further better manage this one special night that they’ve all been waiting for.
