My Favorite Application

My favorite application at the moment is the famous, Facebook application. What I like about this application is that you could share, read, watch, stalk, view, and post almost everything. What makes it good for me is that the application is the source of my happiness when I see funny posts such as memes and watch videos that entertains me. I follow pages that I liked and whenever I’m not doing anything, I’m on Facebook; and I just scroll and scroll to my heart’s content. I think I’ve been using Facebook for over 8 years now. I remember creating an account when I was in my 6th grade and I just post and share anything that I want. I was able to talk to my friends and classmates through this app back then, since Messenger is not yet created and developed at that time. The features on Facebook really developed for the better over the years. I was able to play online games through the app; watch videos, posts pictures, videos, and texts; I could save posts for later use; I use its albums as a backup or dump for the photos and videos stored in my phone to free up some space; and you can create and support pages that create almost any content such as a school’s dashboard, newscasts, foundations, gaming content, and charities. Mainly, what I like about this Facebook application is that I could catch up with my friends, family, relatives, and colleagues lives and stories with what they post and share. It’s a way for us to really connect with each other still keeping in check what’s happening with our lives. We get to share the same experience and able to contribute meaningful to someone else’s lives. This application really bridges lives and relationships regardless of distance and time.
